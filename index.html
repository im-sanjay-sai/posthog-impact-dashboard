How Impact is Measured

Shipping 35% Volume and complexity of code merged. Each PR is scored as log2(lines changed) x log2(files touched) - rewarding meaningful work without over-weighting bulk refactors.
Reviewing 30% Quality of code reviews. Reviews with substantive comments and change requests score higher than simple approvals.
Consistency 20% Weeks active out of the period. Rewards sustained engagement over burst activity.
Reach 15% Breadth of collaboration. Engineers who review many people's code and are reviewed by many score higher.

Why these metrics? Lines of code and commit counts don't capture impact. This model rewards engineers who ship meaningful changes, elevate their team through reviews, show up consistently, and work across organizational boundaries.

Scores are normalized relative to the top contributor in each dimension, so 100 = the highest in the team.
